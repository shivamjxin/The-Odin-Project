Oats

Description

Overnight oats are a convenient and nutritious breakfast option that has gained immense popularity for their simplicity and health benefits. Made by soaking rolled oats in liquid, typically milk or a dairy-free alternative, overnight in the refrigerator, they offer a creamy, ready-to-eat meal by morning. This method not only saves time but also enhances the oats' texture, making them soft and palatable. The beauty of overnight oats lies in their versatility; you can customize them with an array of add-ins such as fruits, nuts, seeds, and sweeteners to suit your taste preferences and nutritional needs.

What sets overnight oats apart is their impressive nutritional profile. They are a rich source of fiber, which aids digestion and keeps you full longer, and provide essential vitamins and minerals like iron, magnesium, and B vitamins. Additionally, they can be tailored to fit various dietary restrictions, making them a suitable choice for vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free diets. Whether you enjoy them plain or loaded with toppings, overnight oats are a delicious and healthy way to kickstart your day.

Ingredients

You need just five ingredients (plus some warm water) to make this super simple pizza crust.
  • Rolled Oats
    ½ cup rolled oats
  • Chocolate Milk
    ⅔ cup Almond Breeze Chocolate Almond milk
  • Powdered Sugar
    2 teaspoons powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
    2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Toasted Almonds
    1 tablespoon toasted almonds
  • Chocolate chips 1 tablespoon mini chocolate chips
  • Flaked Coconut
    1 tablespoon flaked coconut

Steps

  1. In pint jar or at least 12-ounce glass, mix oats, almond milk, powdered sugar, and cocoa powder. Cover; refrigerate at least 8 hours or overnight.
  2. To serve, stir mixture and top with almonds, chocolate chips, and coconut.
